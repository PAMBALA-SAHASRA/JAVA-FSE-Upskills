Community Bulletin

This summer, the city will host a series of open-air movie nights in Central Park, starting on June 5th. Each Friday, residents can bring blankets and enjoy family-friendly films under the stars.

Local artists will showcase their work in an outdoor art fair next month. The fair will feature painting, sculpture, and live art installations, and aims to promote creativity and support independent creators.

The youth center has announced new workshops for teens, focusing on digital art, music production, and robotics. These workshops are free and begin on July 10th with limited seats available.

A community clean-up drive will be held this Saturday. Volunteers are encouraged to bring gloves, water, and a positive attitude. Participants will be assigned to different zones to beautify public spaces.
